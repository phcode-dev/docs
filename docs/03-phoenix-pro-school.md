# Phoenix Pro for Schools & Universities (Free)
A large portion of the Phoenix Code community comes from schools and universities, where students and teachers value ease of use and a low learning curve. Education has always been central to our mission, and we want every student to have access to the full Phoenix Pro feature set without barriers.

For this reason, we provide **Phoenix Pro for free to the entire education ecosystem**, along with deployment tools tailored to classrooms, labs, and student devices.

Schools can deploy Phoenix Pro in two ways:

1. **Email-based automatic activation** (recommended)
2. **Educational Volume License Keys** (for labs, shared devices, and student/teacher-owned devices)

This page explains how schools and universities can obtain free Phoenix Pro access and deploy it across **any devices used within the institution**.

---

## Who Is Eligible?

Phoenix Pro is free for:

* Students and teachers with **.edu** or **.ac.*** email addresses
* Institutions we already recognize and verify
* Schools requesting **Educational Volume Licenses**
* Both **commercial and non-commercial** educational use

Volume licenses may be applied to:

* School-owned lab computers
* Shared classroom devices
* **Student/teacher-owned laptops/home computers** (per administrator distribution)

---

# Option 1: Email-Based Student & Teacher Activation (Instant)

Phoenix Pro is automatically activated for users who log in with:

* A **.edu** email
* A **.ac.*** email
* Any **recognized school domain** already onboarded to Phoenix Code

### **How It Works**

1. Open Phoenix Code
2. Sign in using your school email
3. Phoenix Pro activates instantly
4. No approval or license key required

---

## Schools Without a `.edu` or `.ac.*` Domain

Some schools, especially K-12 institutions, use custom domains.
We support these too.

### To onboard your school domain:

Email **[support@core.ai](mailto:support@core.ai)** with:

* Subject:
  **`[education support] Request to onboard <School Name>`**
* School website URL
* Admin/teacher contact email

Once your domain is approved, all users with that email domain automatically receive Phoenix Pro upon login.

This is suitable for:

## How Volume License Keys Work

* Each license key is **valid for 2 weeks**
* During these 2 weeks, you may activate **unlimited devices**:
  * School-owned devices
  * Lab computers
  * Shared machines
  * **Student/teacher-owned devices (allowed)**
* Once activated, a device stays Phoenix Pro **permanently**
* After the 2-week window, the key expires
* Admins can generate a **new key at any time** for new devices

This model ensures:

* Easy deployment
* Login is optional, no login required on each machine.
* The device now has Phoenix Pro permanently
- School admin may rotate(reissue) keys as needed

# Limitations

### Students without school email and no school onboarding

If a student does not have a school-provided email **and the school is not onboarded**, we currently cannot issue individual student educational licenses.

### Volume licenses must be requested by authorized school representatives

Students cannot request volume licenses directly without school/teacher approval.

# FAQ

### **Can students use the volume license on their personal laptops?**

Yes. Schools may distribute the Education Volume License Key to activate student-owned devices.

### **Do device activations remain permanent?**

Yes. Even after the license key expires, previously activated devices remain Phoenix Pro.

### **Can we issue new volume license keys whenever needed?**

Yes. The admin can generate new 2-week activation keys anytime.

### **Can teachers use Phoenix Pro for personal/professional work?**

### **Is Phoenix Pro free for commercial educational projects?**

Yes. Students and teachers may use Phoenix Pro for any purpose.
